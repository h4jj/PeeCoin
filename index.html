About PeeFinance

PeeSwap is not your average decentralized exchange. We aim to revolutionize the space by introducing new features that have never been thought of before. PeeFinance will harbor a collective of services including a DEX, NFT marketplace, and Transaction data analytics center. We aim to build a fully decentralized ecosystem to provide the best experience for our users in a way that hasn't been accomplished before in the crypto-space. Our team of experts in data analytics, software engineering, and web development will be paving the path to make our vision possible. $PEE will be used to navigate and interact with the different services (dapps) our platform will offer. Our platform, exclusively governed by $PEE, is built around the wants and needs of the crypto community that are yet to be addressed by the current DEX platforms. Lets revolutionize the space together and take a step into advancing upon what the current platforms lack.

The Ultimate One Stop Shop

Due to the abundance of scams happening in the CryptoSpace, there is a need for a regulatory body to exist. PeeFinance will harbor a collective of Dapps that aim to create The Ultimate One Stop shop in a safe environment for all levels of investors. Our mission is to ensure the safety of investors because let's face it, not everyone can read code. That's where we come in by attempting to decrease potential scams as much as possible with our proprietary auditing software alongside other tools and informing users of everything they need to know about before investing in a coin. The platform, governed by the $PEE token, will allow traders to view fully decentralized tokens from announcement to launch and thereafter all under one hub.
